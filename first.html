Three places to visit on your trip to Colombia

Colombia is a fascinating country, full of cultural diversity, unmatched gastronomy and beautiful landscapes. Here you will find hidden paradises full of magic and history that will captivate you. Are you ready to discover them? We present you the places you should know in Colombia!

Guatape

It is a picturesque municipality located in the department of Antioquia, Colombia. It is famous for its vibrant, colorful houses and the main attraction Penol-Guatape, a massive monolithic rock that you can climb and observe the panoramic views of the eservoir and the mountains.
